Who We Are

A Niche Corporate Law Firm Built on Trust & Expertise

Mdyesha Ndema Attorneys (MNA) is a niche corporate law firm that offers a boutique range of products and legal solutions to all its corporate, public and private clients. The focus of the firm is to understand the business needs of our clients in order to provide them with products and solutions that meet their requirements and make a meaningful difference to their businesses.

The firm's distinguishing attribute is the pursuance of a value-adding, solution orientated, personalized service offered to all its clients. We strive to provide a quality service at competitive rates which give us a competitive edge in relation to our competitors operating in the same market.

MNA is a 100% Black-owned and managed firm and a Level 1 BBBEE contributor. We are a proud, strong, and noble profession — sworn in as officers of the court, part of a legal system that our society relies on for justice and fairness.

Our Vision

To offer enduring business solutions to match our clients' needs. We aim to be a critical role player in the corporate and government legal landscape as well as development finance institutions and other State Owned entities (Parastatals).

We abide by our ethical codes of integrity, upholding the highest standards of our profession and applying best practices in all aspects of our business.

Our Mission

To strive to provide high-quality and cost-effective legal services to our clients and to become one of South Africa's most prominent and progressive commercial and corporate governance law firms.

We aim to focus on long-term relationships through growth in the existing and new markets by providing high-quality legal services and striving for excellence in serving our clients.
