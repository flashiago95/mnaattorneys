What areas of law does MNA Attorneys specialise in?

MNA offers a broad range of legal services including Commercial & Corporate Law, Company Secretarial & Governance, Property Law & Conveyancing, Litigation, Entertainment & Sports Law, Labour Law, ICT Law, and more. We serve both private and public sector clients.

How is MNA different from larger law firms?

Unlike large firms where your matter may be handled by junior associates, at MNA your work is personally managed by experienced directors. We offer senior-level expertise at competitive rates, with a personalised, solution-orientated approach.

Does MNA work with government and public entities?

Yes. MNA has extensive experience advising government departments, parastatals, municipalities, and development finance institutions. We are a Level 1 BBBEE contributor and are well-positioned to serve public sector clients.

Where are your offices located?

MNA has offices in Johannesburg (Spaces, Design Quarter, Fourways) and East London (6 Sansom Street, Vincent). We serve clients nationwide and accommodate remote consultations.

How do I book a consultation?

You can reach us by phone at 011 513 3387, by email at info@mnaattorneys.co.za, or through our website contact form. We respond to all enquiries within one business day.
